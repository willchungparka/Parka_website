01 About Us
Parka Capital Management ("PCM") manages real estate and related investments across the US on behalf of its investors. With over 50 years of combined experience, the principals of PCM have a proven track record of successfully acquiring, managing, and investing in a diverse range of real estate sectors including multifamily, manufactured housing communities, and other commercial real estate. The principals have worked together for well over a decade, deploying over $5 billion of capital on behalf of institutional investors and asset owners.

Parka focuses on investing, integrating and improving mobile home communities (MHC) in high growth markets through value-add initiatives and tech enablement. Parka commits to be the leading workforce housing solution provider in the communities it serves.

03 Our Thesis
  • MHC is the solution of choice for affordable housing as the US market struggles to balance limited hosing stock and worsening affordability
  • MHC enables inclusive and equitable home ownership for residents across the income and socioeconomic spectrum
  • High barrier to development limits new supplies and supports sustainable and strong rent growth while remaining below the cost of comparable single family properties
  • Fragmented ownership base provides long runway of acquisition and integration
  • Low turnover tenant bases yielding to defensive cash flow profile with strong crisis era performance during GFC and COVID pandemic
  • Significant variation of operating capabilities calls for a focused national workforce MHC owner operator.
  • PCM’s principals leverage their experiences to drive institutional quality underwriting and investment process
  • PCM taps into its hyper local sourcing network paired with deep national broker relationships
  • In-house development of tech-enabled operational improvements and Life cycle asset enhancements to deliver clear value-add income growth
