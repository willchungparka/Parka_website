01 About Us
Parka Capital Management is a privately-owned company that manages real estate and related investments across the US and overseas. With over 50 years of combined experience, the principals of PCM have a proven track record of successfully acquiring, managing, and investing in a diverse range of real estate assets including multifamily properties, mobile home parks, and other commercial real estate. The principals have worked together for well over a decade, deploying over $5 billion of capital on behalf of institutional investors and asset owners.

03 Our Thesis
  • The US is in a long-term housing crisis of limited supplies and worsening affordability
  • MHC is the solution of choice given its strong affordability (compare to SFR / Multi) and low barrier for home ownership
  • MHC enables inclusive and equitable home ownership for residents across the spectrum
  • High barrier to development limits new supplies and supports strong rent growth
  • Fragmented ownership base provides long runway of acquisition targets
  • Sticky and low turnover tenant bases yielding to defensive cash flow profile
  • Strong downside protection with strong crisis era performance during GFC and COVID pandemic
  • Sustainable rent growth exceeds comparable residential assets while retaining lower per SF rental costs
  • Lack of workforce housing focused national operator shows wide industry margin spreads2
  • Institutional quality underwriting and Investment Process
  • Hyper local sourcing network paired with national broker relationships (sourcing clusters for each country)
  • Tech-enabled Operational Improvements
  • Lifecycle asset management and enhancement
  • Stable Management Team and Aligned Incentives
